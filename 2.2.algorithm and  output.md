2.2 Multiplication Table Generator

Scenario: Print the multiplication table of a number up to 10.

Algorithm:

1. Initialize 'num' and assign a value.

2. Loop i from 1 to 10:
   • Multiply: result = num * i
   • Display the result.

Output:
Enter a number: 5
Multiplication table of given number: 5
5 * 1 = 5
5 * 2 = 10
5 * 3 = 15
5 * 4 = 20
5 * 5 = 25
5 * 6 = 30
5 * 7 = 35
5 * 8 = 40
5 * 9 = 45
5 * 10 = 50
